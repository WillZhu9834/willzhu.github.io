Description

This vibrant Van-Gogh-themed painting depicts serene sunflowers under a starry sky. Inspired by Van Gogh's brushwork and color play, it evokes a sense of warmth and wonder.

Details

  • Artist: Yóng
  • Medium: Acrylic on Canvas
  • Dimensions: 24" x 36"
  • Year: July, 2024
  • Style: Post-Impressionism

Inspiration

I created this as a birthday gift for one of my friends. I was inspaired by Van Gogh's Starry Night and Sunflowers because sunflower is my friend's favoriate flower, and as a big fan of Van Gogh and impressionism, I always try to micmic his works.

This work is named as 'Day&Night', representing the time for stars and sunflowers. If observing this painting, you probably see it in two ways: A painting in a painting. Some sunflowers are placed in front of a starry night painting. Well, it can be also interprated as a night seeing through the window, where you can smell of lasting scent of those thriving sunflowers.

Description

This vibrant night-themed painting depicts a bonfire under a starry sky. Inspired by a campfire and my wonder for the beams in darkness.

Details

  • Artist: Yóng
  • Medium: Acrylic on Canvas
  • Dimensions: 24" x 36"
  • Year: August, 2024
  • Style: Post-Impressionism

Inspiration

This is the first time to create something dark. I had this idea of painting the burning flames in an 'After-sunset' scene. Here you can see the tiny part of the earthline with a hue of reddish orange mixed with pinkish navy blue. That is the last sight of the sun. Short and Amazing.

The sky and tree shadows were fairly easy to manage, but the fire, the highlight of this piece, actually took me of hours to create in details. The sparkling small fires, the floating smoke, the burning woods, the light, and the shadows all contribute to the final presentation of the lively campfire.
